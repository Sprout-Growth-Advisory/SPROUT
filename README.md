# Sprout Growth Systems

## Brand Identity

Sprout Growth Systems is a modern growth systems company focused on helping service-based businesses stop losing leads and recover missed revenue through automation, follow-up systems, and client reactivation.

The brand aesthetic should feel:
- Premium
- Minimal
- Modern
- Professional
- Dark themed
- Black + deep natural green color palette
- Clean UI with smooth animations
- Apple / Linear / modern SaaS inspired

The website should NOT feel like:
- A generic marketing agency
- A loud SMMA
- A cheap automation business
- Overly corporate

It should feel:
- Calm
- Premium
- Organized
- Efficient
- High-value
- System focused

# What Sprout Actually Does

Sprout builds automated customer growth systems for service businesses.

We implement:
- Missed call text-back systems
- Automated lead replies
- Appointment reminder systems
- No-show follow-up automations
- Review request systems
- Dormant client reactivation campaigns
- CRM pipeline systems
- Lead tracking systems
- Customer follow-up workflows

The business is implementation-focused, not consulting-focused.

Sprout is NOT:
- A social media management company
- A branding agency
- A website design agency only
- A vague consulting business

Sprout focuses on operational revenue systems and customer follow-up infrastructure.
