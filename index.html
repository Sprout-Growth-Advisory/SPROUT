The follow-up gap

Service Businesses Lose Revenue In The Follow-Up Gap

Many service-based businesses miss revenue because new inquiries are not followed up with quickly, past clients are not reactivated, and customer touchpoints happen inconsistently.

Sprout builds practical systems that make follow-up visible, repeatable, and easier for the team to use. The goal is a cleaner path from inquiry to booked revenue.

Missed revenue signals

Random tactics cannot fix a scattered follow-up process.
